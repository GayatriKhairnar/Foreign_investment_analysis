    <h1>Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) Dashboard</h1>

    <h2>Overview</h2>
    <p>This repository contains interactive dashboards that provide insights into Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) trends, sector performance, and future projections. The dashboards facilitate a deeper understanding of FDI data through various visualizations and analytical tools.</p>

    <hr>

    <h2>Dashboard 1: FDI Overview</h2>

    <h3>Total Sectors</h3>
    <p>Displays the total number of sectors analyzed for FDI.</p>

    <h3>Top and Bottom Sectors by FDI</h3>

    <h4>Maximum FDI</h4>
    <ul>
        <li>A table showcasing the top 5 sectors with the highest FDI (in crore rupees).</li>
        <li>The service sector emerges as the highest recipient of FDI.</li>
    </ul>

    <h4>Minimum FDI</h4>
    <ul>
        <li>A table highlighting sectors with the lowest FDI.</li>
        <li>Indicates potential areas for investment or improvement.</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Overall FDI Trend</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>A line chart illustrating the overall trend of FDI from 2005 to 2008.</li>
        <li>Shows a significant rise during this period.</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>FDI Growth and Decline</h3>

    <h4>Highest Growth in FDI (Past 5 Years)</h4>
    <p>A heat map displaying sectors with the highest FDI growth over the past five years.</p>

    <h4>Highest Decline in FDI (Past 5 Years)</h4>
    <p>A heat map showcasing sectors experiencing the highest decline in FDI over the same period.</p>

    <h3>Sector Group Filter</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>A filter enabling users to explore insights specific to different sector groups.</li>
        <li>Allows for deeper analysis and comparison.</li>
    </ul>

    <hr>

    <h2>Dashboard 2: Detailed Sector Analysis and Forecast</h2>

    <h3>Sector-wise FDI Treemap</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>A treemap visualization representing FDI distribution across different sectors.</li>
        <li>Offers a comparative view of sector-wise FDI allocation.</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Trend Forecast Graph</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>Provides insights into the predicted trajectory of FDI trends.</li>
        <li>Aids in strategic planning and decision-making.</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Cluster Analysis</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>A cluster chart categorizing sectors into similar clusters based on FDI patterns.</li>
        <li>Reveals underlying trends and relationships between sectors.</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Gantt Chart</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>Visualizes FDI activities over time.</li>
        <li>Offers a comprehensive view of project durations and scheduling.</li>
    </ul>

    <hr>

    <h2>Insights & Benefits</h2>
    <ul>
        <li>Comprehensive analysis of FDI trends and sector performance.</li>
        <li>Identification of high-growth and declining sectors for strategic decision-making.</li>
        <li>Visualization tools enabling effective data exploration.</li>
        <li>Predictive insights for future investment planning.</li>
    </ul>
